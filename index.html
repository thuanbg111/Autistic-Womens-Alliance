AUTISTIC WOMEN’S ALLIANCE

We are a California 501(c)(3) nonprofit public benefit corporation. Our mission is to support and mentor autistic women — providing peer support, skills training, resources navigation, and advocacy to help women thrive with dignity. We champion acceptance, community, and practical supports for autistic women.

Learn more See programs

What We Do

Peer‑led support and practical tools for everyday life.

  • Peer mentoring & support groups
  • Skills & employment readiness workshops
  • Resources navigation & advocacy

Skills & Workshops

Executive‑function tools, workplace skills, self‑advocacy, sensory strategies.

Peer Mentoring

Small‑group mentoring led by autistic women—safe, supportive, and practical.

Resource Navigation

Referrals to healthcare, counseling, benefits, and legal/education supports.
